Introduction

Titan Company Limited, a part of the Tata Group, is one of India's leading lifestyle companies. Established in 1984, Titan has grown to become a household name, especially known for its watches. The company has diversified into various categories, including jewelry, eyewear, and accessories.

  • Titan's watch division offers a wide range of timepieces, from the minimalist and ultra-slim Titan Edge collection to the innovative and stylish Titan Raga. They also have smartwatches and hybrid watches that blend traditional design with modern technology.

    • About Us

    Titan Company Limited, a member of the Tata Group, was established in 1984. Headquartered in Bangalore, India, Titan has grown to be a leading player in the lifestyle and accessory segments. Here are some highlights of the company:

  • Watches: Titan is synonymous with watches in India. The company offers a wide range of watches under various brands, including Titan, Fastrack, Sonata, and luxury brand Xylys. They cater to different customer segments, from affordable to premium timepieces.
  • Jewelry: Titan's jewelry division, Tanishq, is one of India's largest jewelry brands. It offers a wide variety of gold, diamond, and platinum jewelry.
  • Eyewear: Titan Eyeplus is the company's eyewear retail chain, offering a range of frames, lenses, and sunglasses. They also provide eye-testing services in their stores.
  • Fragrances: Titan has ventured into the fragrance industry with its brand Skinn, offering a range of perfumes for men and women.
  • Other Accessories: Titan also offers bags, belts, and wallets through its accessories division.
